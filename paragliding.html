Paragliding

Paragliding 6 Places To Experience Paragliding In Nepal Pokhara. Chapakharka. KotDanda. Godavari. Phulchowki. Bandipur. Cost Of Paragliding In Nepal Paragliding may not be a budget experience for everyone but if you have that thrill inside you, then this is an must experience in Nepal. The paragliding cost in Nepal can vary as the prices are not fixed and it may differ according to the season. Paragliding is an adventure sport which solely depends on the weather and the gliding season in Nepal starts from November to February. But the ideal time to experience this thrilling sport is between November to December. So fight your fear this time, plan a trip to Nepal and set out to grab on the opportunity to experience the thrill of paragliding in Nepal amidst the picturesque views and live the best time of your life!
